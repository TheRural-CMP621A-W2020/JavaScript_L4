Student Exercise(s)

  1. Create a web page that has three different products. When you click on an image of the product, it will show you a larger view of the product. It will also in a box beside the enlarged picture it will show a price and number in stock. Use random generated numbers (between 10 and 100) for the price and random generated numbers (between 5 and 20) for the number in stock. It is ok if the price and number in stock change each time the image is clicked.
